# AIXONTRA Analysis Features - User Guide

## Overview

AIXONTRA now includes powerful AI-powered analysis tools to help you create hit songs. These tools analyze your lyrics in real-time and provide actionable feedback to improve your music.

## Features

### 1. Lyric Quality Score (0-100)

Analyzes your lyrics across four key dimensions:

#### **Emotion** (0-100)
- Measures emotional depth and impact
- Detects emotional keywords (love, pain, joy, etc.)
- Checks for personal connection (use of "I", "you", "we")
- Bonus points for metaphors and questions
- **Goal**: 70+ for strong emotional connection

#### **Originality** (0-100)
- Evaluates vocabulary diversity
- Detects clich√©s and overused phrases
- Rewards unique word combinations
- Analyzes creative compound words
- **Goal**: 70+ for fresh, unique lyrics

#### **Imagery** (0-100)
- Counts sensory words (visual, auditory, tactile)
- Detects color words and nature imagery
- Rewards vivid adjectives and descriptive language
- **Goal**: 70+ for engaging, visual lyrics

#### **Coherence** (0-100)
- Checks structural consistency
- Analyzes line length uniformity
- Rewards proper section labeling ([Verse], [Chorus])
- Evaluates repetition balance
- **Goal**: 70+ for well-structured lyrics

#### **Poetic Devices Detected**
- Metaphor
- Simile
- Alliteration
- Repetition
- Rhyme
- Personification

### 2. Flow Analysis

Analyzes how well your lyrics flow with the music:

#### **Syllable Count**
- Total syllables in your lyrics
- Average syllables per line
- Syllable distribution graph

#### **Flow Score** (0-100)
- Measures syllable consistency
- Checks BPM matching (ideal syllables per line)
- Analyzes rhythm patterns
- **Goal**: 60+ for good flow

#### **Ideal Syllables per BPM**
- 60-89 BPM: 6 syllables per line
- 90-119 BPM: 8 syllables per line
- 120-139 BPM: 10 syllables per line
- 140+ BPM: 12 syllables per line

#### **Rhyme Scheme**
- Displays detected rhyme pattern (AABB, ABAB, etc.)
- Shows which lines rhyme together

### 3. Hit Potential Meter (0-100)

Predicts commercial success potential:

#### **Hook Catchiness** (0-100)
- Analyzes chorus/hook memorability
- Checks hook length (ideal: 4-12 words)
- Rewards repetition and simple language
- **Goal**: 80+ for highly catchy

#### **Hook Timing** (0-100)
- When does the hook first appear?
- **Perfect**: Within first 30 seconds
- **Good**: Within first 40%
- **Late**: After 50% of song
- **Goal**: 90+ (hook within 30 seconds)

#### **Repetition** (0-100)
- How many times does the chorus repeat?
- **Optimal**: 3-4 times
- **Acceptable**: 2 times
- **Too few**: 1 time
- **Too many**: 5+ times
- **Goal**: 100 (exactly 3-4 choruses)

#### **Energy** (0-100)
- Measures energy level of lyrics
- Detects high-energy words (go, jump, dance, fire)
- Considers BPM (120+ BPM = higher energy)
- Checks for exclamation marks
- **Goal**: 70+ for energetic songs

#### **Viral Moment**
- Detects potential 15-30 second TikTok/social media clip
- Shows timestamp (e.g., 0:45-1:05)
- Scores viral potential (0-100)
- Usually the first chorus

## How to Use

### Step 1: Set Your BPM
1. Select your genres first
2. BPM will auto-adjust to the ideal tempo
3. Fine-tune BPM manually if needed (60-180)

### Step 2: Generate Lyrics
1. Enter your creative prompt
2. Check genre recommendations
3. Select genres and mood
4. Click "Generate Lyrics"

### Step 3: Analyze Your Lyrics
1. Click "Show Lyric Analysis" button
2. Review all three analysis cards:
   - Lyric Quality Score
   - Flow Analysis
   - Hit Potential Meter
3. Read improvement suggestions

### Step 4: Edit and Improve
1. Edit lyrics based on suggestions
2. Add section tags: [Verse 1], [Chorus], [Bridge]
3. Adjust syllable count to match BPM
4. Make hook catchier and move it earlier
5. Repeat analysis to see improvements

### Step 5: Optimize for Hit Potential
- **Hook placement**: Move chorus to first 30 seconds
- **Hook length**: Keep it 4-12 words
- **Repetition**: Repeat chorus 3-4 times
- **Energy**: Add energetic words and exclamations
- **Structure**: Use clear section labels

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Low Emotion Score
**Problem**: Generic, impersonal lyrics
**Solution**: 
- Add "I", "you", "we" pronouns
- Use emotional keywords (love, pain, joy)
- Include questions to engage listener
- Add metaphors and comparisons

### Low Originality Score
**Problem**: Too many clich√©s
**Solution**:
- Replace common phrases with unique expressions
- Use more diverse vocabulary
- Avoid "baby", "yeah", "oh", "la la"
- Create your own metaphors

### Low Flow Score
**Problem**: Inconsistent line lengths
**Solution**:
- Count syllables per line
- Aim for ¬±2 syllables from average
- Match ideal syllables for your BPM
- Balance long and short lines

### Low Hit Potential
**Problem**: Hook comes too late
**Solution**:
- Move chorus to verse 2 position or earlier
- Start with hook after short intro
- Repeat chorus 3-4 times minimum

### No Viral Moment Detected
**Problem**: No standout section
**Solution**:
- Make your first chorus extra catchy
- Use repetition in the hook
- Add memorable phrase with exclamation
- Keep it simple and singable
